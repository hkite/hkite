[12] RTBF法理依据

如果只是根据Mario Consteja Gonzalez案的判决来看的话，RTBF的法理依据是人身自由权和稳私权，是衍生出来的一种权利。并说“It is not necessary that the information is prejudicial to the data subject.”。所以，“不要被过去影响”只能算是这个权利比较重要的方面，而非全部。

[17] PDPO

其实这个规定是有些历史背景的。PDPO有个原则是lawful and fair collection. 其主要目的是防止数据收集者滥用数据。香港在PDPO改革之前常有的现象就是我们时不时会收到营销电话，并且营销人员能直接叫出我们的名字，而我们并不知道他们是怎么得到我们的数据的。所以这个原则的一个重要目的是为了对营销进行监管。
“The amendment of PDPO allows the ordinance has a stricter and clearer supervision on direct marketing and sales or disclosure of personal data. After the reform, the data subject must be informed about the kinds of personal data that will be used, the classes of persons to which the data will be provided or sold and where the data will be used. This allows the data subject to know their data is being used instead of being deceived.”
